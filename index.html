Astrit Tola

Postdoctoral Scholar · Department of Mathematics · Florida State University

I work at the intersection of Topological Data Analysis, Machine Learning, and AI, with applications spanning drug discovery, biological networks, and cybersecurity. I lead TopoML&AI @ FSU — a research group designing topology-aware AI methods that make modern ML more interpretable, robust, and grounded in mathematical structure.

Previously, I completed my Ph.D. in Mathematics at the University of Texas at Dallas (2020–2025), advised by Prof. Baris Coskunuzer. My dissertation, "Graph Representation Learning with Topological Data Analysis," developed new topological methods for learning expressive, interpretable representations of graphs. I currently work with Prof. Malbor Asllani at FSU.

June 2026

New Grant ReliaQuest Innovation Challenge Award

Excited to share that our proposal, topocorr-kg.html"TopoCorr-KG: Topology-Aware Retrieval and Correlation Across Disparate Cybersecurity Knowledge Graphs," has been selected for the ReliaQuest Innovation Challenge at Florida State University. The project develops AI-driven methods for retrieving and correlating information across separate cybersecurity knowledge graphs, with the goal of improving cyber threat analysis and decision-making.

Looking forward to collaborating with Co-PI Malbor Asllani, with Ece Karacam (GTA) and Sebastian Powers (UTA) joining the team. Thank you to ReliaQuest and FSU for supporting this initiative!
